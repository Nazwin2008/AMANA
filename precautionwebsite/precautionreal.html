How to Respond

1

Listen

When the alert sounds on your phone, stay calm and listen to the instructions provided by NCEMA.

2

Locate

Immediately move to your pre-selected safe room. Stay away from glass and balconies.

3

Liaise

Wait for the "All Clear" before leaving. Check on family via SMS to keep phone lines open.

Strict Policy: No Recording

Filming missile interceptions is illegal. It exposes defense locations and puts you at risk of falling debris.

🏠 Identifying Safe Zones

  • Interior hallways with no windows.
  • Underground basements.
  • Rooms with multiple walls between you and the outside.

📡 Official Sources

NCEMA Official X (Twitter) UAE Ministry of Interior

💡 Myth vs. Fact

Myth: "I should hide in my car to drive away."

Fact: Cars are not safe shelters. Stay inside a sturdy building until the all-clear is given.
